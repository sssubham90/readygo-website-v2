Puri, the spiritual capital of Odisha is also known as Jagannath Dham (Abode of Lord  Jagannath) and is one amongst the four holy dhams of Hinduism. It is located at a distance around 60km from Bhubaneswar. Puri is famous for its ancient temples, architechural  splendour, beautiful sea view and pleasant climate. Washed by the waters of the Bay of Bengal and the city is embraced by the golden beaches which attracts tourist from all over the world.

Places to visit in and around Puri

Jagannath Temple

Being one amongst the famous Char Dhams, it is the most visited pilgrimage site in Odisha.The Mahaprasad available here is too good to be missed.

Puri Beach

It is the most visited beach on the eastern coast of India.It is perfect for peaceful strolling and enjoying both the sunrise and sunset.

Puri-Konark Marine Drive

The stunning Puri-Konark Marine Drive is full of several amazing sights along the way including views of the river and the sea. You can also stop over at the chandrabhaga beach to enjoy some breeze.

Konark Sun Temple

An architectural marvel and symbol of India's heritage, the Sun Temple is a very famous tourist destination.It is located about 35 km from puri and is worth a visit.

Balighai Beach

Much famous for the olive ridley turtles and for the migrating birds, it is a pristine sea beach which attracts lots of tourists throughout the year.

Chilika Lake

Asia's largest brackish water lagoon spread over 1100 sq km, Chilika Lake is home to a phenomenal variety of birds. One can enjoy bird watching and a range of water sports here.
